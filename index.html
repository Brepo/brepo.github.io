Readybox

The fastest consumer 3D printer ever.

3D printers are considered to be one of the best inventions of modern times.
They do incredible things. They create magic.
Yet in order to be a regular household tool, these printers need to be able to finish their job in minutes, not hours.

Very few 3D printer companies have attempted to fix this problem.
Most of the time, their focus is either on cost or aesthetics.
The very few companies that attempt to maximize speed simply consider it just an additional feature.

Readybox has solved this problem.

It is completely designed for speed-
Everything else is an added feature.

Faster Extrusion

A new, high flow extruder design coupled with a clog-free nozzle means that filament can be extruded more than 6 times faster than existing 3D printers without the risk of clogging or damaging the printer's mechanics.

Smoother Movement

A low friction movement system allows the print head to move incredibly fast, while still retaining all the accuracy of other, slower printers.

Light Design

A small, light print head helps the printer achieve extremely high accelerations, allowing it to take full advantage of other hardware and software improvements and achieve the fastest speed in the market.

Better Tech

A next generation 32bit ARM based controller board brings the software and hardware together to create a seamless, easy printing experience.

What else makes Readybox unique?

Readybox was designed to achieve what other existing 3D printers couldn't.
This refers not only to faster prints, but also to eliminating the common challenges of printer calibration and maintenance.

Easier use

Due to its auto-leveling capabilities, all you need to do is press a button and it will calibrate automatically. It also utilizes a clog-free nozzle, meaning that the filament will never get stuck (which is a huge problem with almost every other consumer 3D printer).
